TechTrend - Site Plan

E-Commerce Product Showcase

Site Name

TechTrend

This name represents a modern e-commerce platform focused on showcasing the latest technology products. I chose it because it conveys both the technological nature of the products and the trendy, up-to-date selection available.

Optional domain availability: techtrend.shop

Site Purpose

The TechTrend website will provide:

  • A showcase for featured technology products on the homepage with promotional banners
  • A comprehensive products page with grid layout and filtering options
  • Detailed product pages with descriptions, images, and purchase options
  • An interactive shopping cart with JavaScript functionality for quantity updates

Scenarios

Scenario 1

Question: What are the specifications and features of this laptop model?

Content Solution: The product detail page will provide comprehensive specifications, high-quality images, and detailed feature descriptions.

Scenario 2

Question: How can I filter products by price range and category?

Content Solution: The products page will include filter options for price, category, brand, and other relevant attributes to help users find what they're looking for.
